A Vintage Clothing Store located in South Pasadena!

Our store opened January of 2022. We're proud to offer reasonably priced, carefully curated vintage, retro and modern clothing and accessories. We are constantly hunting for unique, quality pieces to delight all our clients. South Pasadena Vintage is fueled by a passion for fashion and driven to be kind to the environment. Shopping at South Pasadena Vintage lets our customers satisfy both needs. Drop by often and get in on the fun. Our store is comprised of many different sellers with their own individual sections and pricing. Vintage is a popular expression that is frequently used to refer to all old styles of clothing. In its modern use, it often indicates that the item is at least 20 years old, while items a century old or more can be considered antique. Today vintage dressing encompasses accessories, mixing vintage garments with new, as well as creating an ensemble of various styles and periods.
